What is Scrooge McDuck Coin?

Scrooge McDuck Coin (MCDUCK)is a decentralized finance coin built on the BEP-20 platform.

The main idea is to create coins based on cartoon characters whom likes to make a lot of money and then integrate them to own Cartoon Protocol blockchain.

Also we aim to facilitate global adoption of our cryptocurrency by introducing Debit Cards and Smart Wallets that dynamically interact with related programs within our block-chain from 2022.

Currently our primary purpose is to launch our first coin, make the biggest hype and launch our project to the moon together with you.
